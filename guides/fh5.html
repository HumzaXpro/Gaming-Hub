FORZA HORIZON GUIDES

Vehicle Selection: Choose your preferred car from a wide range of vehicles, including sports cars, supercars, off-road vehicles, and more.
Difficulty Settings: Adjust the difficulty settings to suit your preferences, including driving assists, opponent skill level, and race length.

Exploration and Open World:

Horizon Festival: Explore the open world of Forza Horizon, which features a diverse landscape including cities, countryside, mountains, and beaches.
Discover Points of Interest: Seek out points of interest such as scenic vistas, landmarks, and hidden collectibles scattered throughout the map.

Events and Races:

Race Types: Participate in a variety of events and races, including circuit races, point-to-point races, off-road races, and showcase events.
Event Types: Compete in traditional races, time trials, drift challenges, and more to earn credits, experience points, and rewards.

Customization and Tuning:

Car Customization: Customize your vehicle with a wide range of options, including paint jobs, decals, rims, and body kits.
Tuning: Fine-tune your car's performance to suit different race conditions and driving styles. Adjust settings such as tire pressure, gear ratios, suspension, and aerodynamics.

Progression and Rewards:

Earn Credits and XP: Complete races, challenges, and events to earn credits and experience points (XP). Use credits to purchase new cars, upgrades, and customization items.
Level Up: Level up your Horizon Life progression to unlock new events, cars, and features. Progress through different tiers of the Horizon Festival to access more prestigious events and rewards.

Drifting and Skill Challenges:

Drift Zones: Test your drifting skills in designated drift zones scattered throughout the map. Score points by maintaining long drifts and achieving high speeds.
Skill Chains: Build skill chains by performing stunts, drifting, near misses, and other skillful maneuvers while driving. Keep your chain going to earn bonus points and rewards.

Online Multiplayer:

Online Modes: Join online multiplayer sessions to race against other players, compete in challenges, and participate in cooperative events.
Horizon Life: Interact with other players in the shared open world of Forza Horizon. Join clubs, share liveries, and participate in community events and challenges.

Seasons and Events:

Dynamic Seasons: Experience dynamic changes in weather and seasons that affect driving conditions and gameplay. Adapt your driving style to rain, snow, and other weather conditions.
Seasonal Events: Take part in seasonal events and challenges that rotate regularly, offering unique rewards and experiences based on the current season.

Community and Content Creation:

Creative Hub: Access the Creative Hub to discover user-generated content, including custom races, liveries, and tuning setups.
Content Creator: Use the Content Creator tools to design and share your own custom races and events with the Forza Horizon community.

Achievements and Challenges:

Complete Challenges: Pursue in-game challenges and achievements to test your skills and unlock rewards. Challenge yourself to achieve high scores, complete specific races, and master different driving disciplines.

GTA V GUIDE

Character Selection: Choose between three protagonists: Michael, Franklin, and Trevor. Each has their own unique abilities and storyline.
Controls: Familiarize yourself with the controls for your platform (PC, console). This includes movement, driving, shooting, and interacting with objects.

Main Storyline:

Missions: Progress through the main storyline by completing missions assigned to each character. These missions involve a variety of activities including heists, car chases, shootouts, and more.
Character Switching: Take advantage of the ability to switch between protagonists during missions. This allows you to strategize and use each character's abilities to your advantage.

Exploration and Activities:

Open World: Explore the vast city of Los Santos and the surrounding countryside. Take part in various activities such as:
Side Missions: Engage in side missions and activities such as street races, drug dealing, and property management.
Hobbies and Pastimes: Enjoy recreational activities like golf, tennis, yoga, and darts.
Random Events: Encounter random events and scenarios while exploring the world. These can range from helping civilians in need to thwarting criminal activities.

Money and Assets:

Income Sources: Earn money through completing missions, participating in activities, investing in the stock market, and more.
Properties: Invest in properties such as businesses and real estate to generate passive income over time.
Heists: Plan and execute elaborate heists to earn substantial sums of money. Choose crew members wisely and prepare for intense action sequences.

Customization and Upgrades:

Vehicles: Customize and upgrade your vehicles with paint jobs, performance enhancements, and modifications.
Weapons: Purchase and upgrade weapons to improve their performance and unlock new features.
Character Abilities: Improve your character's abilities through training and practice. Each character has unique skills that can be upgraded over time.

Online Multiplayer (GTA Online):

Create a Character: Create your own character and customize their appearance.
Activities: Participate in various multiplayer activities such as races, deathmatches, and cooperative missions.
Businesses and Organizations: Start your own criminal empire by purchasing businesses, forming organizations, and engaging in illegal activities with other players.

Tips and Strategies:

Save Regularly: Save your progress frequently to avoid losing significant progress.
Plan Ahead: Before undertaking missions or activities, consider your approach and gather necessary resources and equipment.
Watch Your Wanted Level: Avoid attracting too much police attention, as high wanted levels can lead to intense chases and confrontations.

Community Resources:

Online Guides: Utilize online guides, forums, and communities to seek advice, share strategies, and discover hidden secrets within the game.
